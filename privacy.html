Privacy Policy

How Tucson Pool Pal collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: May 2026

Tucson Pool Pal ("we," "us," or "our") is a locally owned pool service business based in Tucson, Arizona. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you fill out a contact form or request a quote. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Details about your pool or the service you're requesting

We do not collect payment information through our website. All service payments are handled separately and directly.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • Schedule and provide pool services
  • Communicate with you about your service
  • Send occasional updates about our services if you have opted in

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form Submissions

Our contact forms are processed through Formspree, a third-party form service. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted securely to Formspree and then forwarded to us. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io.

Google Maps and Google Business Profile

Our business is listed on Google Maps and Google Business Profile. If you interact with our Google listing, Google may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policy. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or analytics software. We do not monitor your browsing behavior on our site.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at kevin@tucsonpoolpal.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
